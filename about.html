About

Gitner connects early-stage startup companies looking for co-founders with other entrepreneurs and vice versa. What makes us different is that we are focused on the local.

There are websites that claim to connect you to cofounders but what you also get is a global ecosystem with unknown variables, and you're rolling the dice in terms of the quality of people you connect with. 

All members on Gitner are already part of an existing entrepreneurial institution like YSpace's Incubator, or are sandbox members from programs such as the DMZ Sandbox. Quality over quantity is our motto when it comes to introducing people and creating lifetime relationships.
